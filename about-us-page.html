About Us

We’re a team of anime lovers just like you, and we believe anime should be something everyone can enjoy without any barriers. That’s why we created this free streaming platform. So, fans all over the world can dive into their favorite series whenever they want, completely free. No sign-ups, no fees, just anime.

We’ve built a massive collection of over 200 episodes and movies, and we’re always adding more. Whether you’re into action-packed shonen, heartwarming slice-of-life, or mind-bending sci-fi, we’ve got something for everyone.

But this site isn’t just about watching anime. It’s about bringing fans together. Through our Facebook group and Anime Arcadia Community website, we’ve created a community where you can share recommendations, discuss episodes, and geek out over the latest anime trends. We’re proud of how tight-knit our community has become, and we’d love for you to be a part of it!

Our Mission

We’re not here to make money. Our mission is to make anime available to everyone. We understand how frustrating it can be when you want to watch a show but can’t access it. That’s why we’re committed to creating a user-friendly platform where anyone can enjoy anime anytime, anywhere for free.

Do you love anime as much as we do? If you're the type of person who’s always recommending shows, staying up late for the latest episode, or talking about anime non-stop, we’d love to have you on board as a free contributor!

Who We’re Looking For
  • Anime Fans Who Want to Help: Whether it's finding the best new series to add to our site or suggesting hidden masterpieces, your knowledge is super valuable!
  • Social Media Enthusiasts: If you’re great at creating posts, keeping conversations going, and connecting with people, you can help us grow our Facebook group and spread the word.
  • Writers & Creators: Love writing reviews, creating fan art, or making lists of the best anime moments? We’d love to feature your work and give you a place to shine.
  • Tech-Savvy Folks: If you’re good with websites, troubleshooting, or even just organizing things, your skills can help us keep the platform running smoothly.

What You’ll Get
  • Be Part of Something Cool: You’ll get to work with a bunch of fellow anime fans who are just as passionate as you are.
  • Creative Freedom: Have an idea for the site or the community? We want to hear it! You'll have room to contribute in ways that make sense for you.
  • Experience and Growth: Whether you’re looking to gain experience in content creation, community management, or tech, we can help you build those skills.
  • Access to Anime Goodness: You’ll always be in the loop on the latest updates and content on our platform, plus you’ll get to contribute to something meaningful.
